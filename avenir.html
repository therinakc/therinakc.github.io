Research

Defining our Target Audience & User Personas:

At first, we mainly focused on new and in-coming immigrants and refugees who struggle with finding jobs in Canada due to factors such as their educational background or language barriers. We did this as this was the problem and statistics presented in the original case challenge. We then created two user personas based on this target audience.

However, as we went through our design process and finalized our features (and as you will see later in this case study), we realized that our target audience can be opened up to everyone in the job search market, not only immigrants and refugees.

This ultimately helped us to define our problem statement.

How might we help new Canadians in their professional journeys and make sure that job-searching is equal for everybody living in Canada regardless of their backgrounds?

Chrome Extension

The main purpose of the chrome extension is to allow users to easily save job postings as they are browsing job boards and easily compare their resume without having to go back to the Avenir site!

Features

From our design thinking process and research, here are the final main features of Avenir we chose to include:

Resume Review

Utilizing our AI services, get detailed feedback on your resume's impact and ATS compliance.

Save & Translate Job Postings

Save any job postings you come across on job boards and then translate it to any language.

Mentorship Support

Unsure about what skills you need for specific jobs and how you can get hired? Find a professional in the field and book a mentorship session with them.
