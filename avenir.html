For the Western Founders Network Product Design Sprint. my team and I created Avenir, a multi-platform application that acts as an all-in-one career helper and increases the chance of landing your dream job.

Problem

From the case challenge, we were given statistics regarding immigrants and poverty in Canada. In 2021, 23% of the Canadian population consisted of immigrants. It’s great that this number is high, but the current system doesn’t adequately support the people coming in. 36% of new immigrants and refugees live in poverty as a result of this system. About the Project

Immigrants and refugees struggle with job searching in a new country as a result of cultural differences in professional standards and lack of community and professional connections.

For the 48 hours we were given to work on our solution, we were given the problem of

How might we help new Canadians in their professional journeys and make sure that job-searching is equal for everybody living in Canada regardless of their backgrounds?

Research

Defining our Target Audience & User Personas:

At first, we mainly focused on new and in-coming immigrants a nd refugees who struggle with finding jobs in Canada due to factors such as their educational background or language barriers. We did this as this was the problem and statistics presented in the original case challenge. We then created two user personas based on this target audience. Sophia and Ronaldo.

However, as we went through our design process and finalized our features (and as you will see later in this case study), we realized that our target audience can be opened up to everyone in the job search market, not only immigrants and refugees.

Competitive Analysis

With the limited time we had, we mainly focused our research on competitive analysis as many of the features we wanted to include were present in a few platforms. Our group also comprised of immigrants who have experienced the difficulty in job-seeking, so we considered what would’ve been nice to have / what we used when going through this process.
